Bank Customer Churn Analysis — SQL

A retail bank was losing customers without knowing which segments
were most at risk — I analysed 10,000 customer records to find out.

Using MS SQL Server and VS Code, I wrote 5 annotated queries
covering geographic segmentation, product holdings, age groups,
balance ranking, and complaint history. The dataset included
18 columns with variables specific to banking — including a
complaint flag that turned out to be the strongest predictor
in the entire dataset.

Key Finding: Customers who filed a complaint had a 99.5% churn
rate versus 0.1% for those who had not — making complaint history
a near-certain predictor of customer exit.

Tools: MS SQL Server · VS Code · GitHub · CTEs · Window Functions
